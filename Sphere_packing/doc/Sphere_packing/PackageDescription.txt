\cgalPkgDesc{
The Sphere Packing provides a method to densely fill a closed triangular mesh with preferably large non-overlapping spheres. The algorithm proceeds by locating potential positions for large spheres based on a grid in parallel on GPU. In each iteration the largest spheres are inserted and the method terminates when a target number or coverage is attained or spheres above a certain radius can no longer be inserted.}

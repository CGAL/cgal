\begin{ccRefConcept}{AlgebraicKernel_d_1} 

\ccDefinition

The \ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1} concept is meant to provide the
algebraic functionalities on univariate polynomials of general degree
$d$ necessary as a basis for the curved kernel.

A model of \ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1} must provide:

\ccTypes

\ccNestedType{Coefficient}{
A model of \ccc{IntegralDomain} and \ccc{RealEmbeddable} 
}

\ccNestedType{Polynomial_1}{
A model of \ccc{Polynomial_1}, 
where \ccc{CGAL::Polynomial_traits_d<Polynomial_1>::Innermost_coefficient}
is \ccc{Coefficient} 
} 

\ccNestedType{Algebraic_real_1}{A model of the concept
\ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::AlgebraicReal_1}}
\ccGlue
\ccNestedType{Boundary}{A model of the concept
\ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::Boundary}}
\ccGlue
\ccNestedType{Algebraic_real_traits}{A model of the concept
\ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::AlgebraicRealTraits_1}}

\ccHeading{Functors}

\ccNestedType{Make_square_free_1}{A model of the concept

\ccDefinition

Concept to represent the roots of a system of three equations of degree 2
in three variables \ccc{x}, \ccc{y} and \ccc{z} that are models of concept
\ccc{AlgebraicKernelForSpheres::PolynomialForSpheres_2_3}.
